Employment

St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy, a private Catholic School in the Diocese of Sacramento, will open this August. Our mission is to provide a quality Catholic education for families wanting the integration of their faith promulgated into all aspects of the lives of their children. We are looking for highly qualified teachers and support staff to fill available positions. Successful applicants will have extensive experience and success in environments that have high expectations, a demand for team-oriented collaboration, and a willingness to be open to learn new methods, teaching styles, and new skills.

A letter of interest and resume should be sent to:
Laura Allen, principal
1351 58th Street
Sacramento, CA  95819

Deadline is February 26, 2010

 

Positions available:

Teacher (Grades Kindergarten - 8th)

In addition to self-contained classroom teachers, specialists will be needed for art, music (vocal and instrumental), theatre arts, science, physical education, special education, speech and language.

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, current California Teaching Credential or currently in a program to obtain one. Master’s Degree preferred. Must be strong in curriculum development and assessment.

Instructional Assistant (Grade Kindergarten – 4th )

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, B.A/B.S degree in liberal studies or other similar area; interested in pursuing a teaching credential.

Preschool Teacher ( 3-4 year old program and a transition kindergarten)

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, Early Childhood Certification and/or California Teaching Credential.

Extension Director

Responsible for supervision of before and after school extension staff and program development.

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, B.A/B.S degree in liberal studies, psychology, child development or similar area.

Secretary

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, B.A/B.S degree preferred, bilingual, office skills, telephone, and computer skills a must.

Bookkeeper

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, B.A. degree in accounting, business management or related area. MBA or CPA preferred.

Plant Manager

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, AA Degree preferred; supervisory experience needed; strong maintenance ability, strong organizational skills needed.

Custodian

Requirements: Practicing Catholic, AA degree preferred; cleaning and maintenance experience required, ability to work independently and take pride in work.
