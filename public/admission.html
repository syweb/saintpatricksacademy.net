Admission

Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy is now accepting applications for the 2010-2011 school year. Applications may be picked up at John Paul II School or St. Patrick School, printed from the PDFs displayed on this website, or by e-mailing lallen@saintpatricks.net. When completed, please return the application to the school office along with copies of your child's:

  • Official Birth Certificate
  • Immunization Records
  • Baptismal Certificate (if Catholic)
  • Last Report Card (for grade 1-8)
  • $25 Application Fee

 
Priority for admission is given to active, supporting members of St.Rose, St. Anne, All Hallows, St. Peter and Immaculate Conception parishes. Catholics who are supporting, active and practicing members of another Catholic parish, as well as non-Catholics, will be considered for admission.

Reserved seats for current St. Patrick
and John Paul II School students

Due to the upcoming closure of John Paul II Elementary School at the end of the spring semester, and the transition of St. Patrick School to Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy in Fall 2010, the Academy is committed to providing a seamless transition for all currently enrolled students.

  • Students will not be tested as assessments and files will be automatically transferred.
  • Registration fee will be reduced by 50% by bringing another family with you to St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy that is not currently at St. Patrick’s or John Paul II.
  • All application fees are waived.

Application Process

1. Applications may be picked up at John Paul II School or St. Patrick School or by e-mailing lallen@saintpatricks.net.

2. One application needs to be filled out for each child that will be attending St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy this fall.

3. Guaranteed enrollment applications will be accepted beginning January 19, 2010 through March 31, 2010. Applications can be mailed to:
St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy
Laura Allen
5945 Franklin Blvd.
Sacramento, CA 95824

4. Beginning April 1, 2010, we will begin the registration process. Financial assistance applications will be available at that time. More information will be sent to you regarding registration, but some information that may help with your planning may be:

  • St. Patrick School students and John Paul II students have a seat reserved for them at ST. Patrick SUCCEED Academy.
  • All application fees are waived for John Paul II and St. Patrick families.
  • Your current tuition will not be increased.
  • Your child(ren) will not need to take entrance tests as assessments and files will be transferred seamlessly.
  • The registration fee will be $250 per child.
  • You can reduce your registration fee by 50% by bringing another family with you to St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy that is not currently at St. Patrick’s or John Paul II.

5. For questions and/or concerns, please contact Laura Allen at lallen@saintpatricks.net or call the school office at (916) 421-4963 and leave a message for her to call you.
