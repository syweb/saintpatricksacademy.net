Location

Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy is centrally located near five South Sacramento parish communities. Opening Fall 2010 on the current St. Patrick School campus on Franklin Boulevard, teachers and students will enjoy a newly renovated, safe and nurturing environment within which to teach, learn and grow.
