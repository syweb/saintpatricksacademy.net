Raley’s Quality of Life Cards

These cards give us 1-4 percent from your total purchases to our school from Raley’s, Bel Air and Food Source. You must register the cards through the internet and it is no cost to you. If you do not have internet access someone else or Diana Stevens can help you do that. You can give cards to family members too. If you would like to request a card please either e-mail or call Diana Stevens at mamommy4@aol.com or 916-629-9270. Thank you for your support!
