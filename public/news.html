Reverse Raffle!

St. Patrick’s SUCCEED Academy Parent Club is proud to present the Reverse Raffle! The Reverse Raffle is a fun event and an even better opportunity to build on the community spirit that is the heart of our school by participating through assistance and/or donations.

Earn parent hours!

Parents/Guardians will receive 5 parent hours for each food, beverage, or spirits vendor that commits to participating in our event.

Raffle prizes!

Help is also needed to solicit raffle prizes for the Reverse Raffle as 1 in every 10 tickets pulled will win a prize! Please see the attached donation letter in the PRIDE envelope.

We feel that this is an especially important event for every parent and guardian of St. Patrick’s SUCCEED Academy to attend because of the intimacy and excitement the event brings. What is EYE?

EYE is our Express Yourself Experience that is integrated throughout the school day of your child(ren). The arts (music, art expression, theatre, dance) are so important to the development of your children. Students involved in the arts enhance their academic abilities, stay focused on school work, and stay in school!

Your students have been working hard on their artwork, dances, dramatic performances, and both choral and instrumental pieces to be presented at our first annual Spring Arts Festival. This year’s festival will be on Friday, March 18th at 7:00 p.m. More information about the evening’s events will be coming home soon. For now, please put the date on your calendar as your child will have artwork displayed and will be performing.
