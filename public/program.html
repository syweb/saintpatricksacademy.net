Programs/Curriculum

Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy offers an excellent Catholic education focused on academic and spiritual formation for success in the 21st century.

Curriculum centered on spiritual development

The St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy curriculum is thoroughly integrated with the Catholic Faith and the realization that Christ is ever-present in our daily lives. Our primary goal is to assist students in living the Gospel message throughout their daily activities and to share the Good News with their family and community.

Academic success for all learners

Young hearts and minds are prepared for success in college-prep high school at our Pre-K-8th academy. The Academy’s credentialed teachers are focused on teaching to their strengths. Classes in the arts, sciences, and technology are woven within all academic areas to address the needs of all students. For students needing additional assistance or more challenging opportunities in language arts and in math, credentialed teachers and qualified staff are available to teach them utilizing the latest in research and technology to assess and provide necessary interventions wherever needed.

Helping children access true talents

Recognizing that God gives every child unique gifts and talents, students have the opportunity to discover who they are. Comprehensive “hands-on” programs explore various disciplines with equal emphasis:

  • Literature, science and technology
  • Fine arts including music, choir, band, orchestra, guitar, art, dance and drama
  • Recreational and competitive athletics

Before and after school extension

Before and after school care is provided for families needing these services. Morning Extension opens at 7:00 a.m. and closes in the afternoon at 6:00 p.m. Students are given the opportunity to begin their homework, work on arts and crafts projects, and interact in social activities that enhance the total St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy experience.

A mission to help every child succeed

“Your child’s readiness for the 21st century — for jobs we don’t even know about yet — starts with discovering who they are. That’s when they become excited about math, science, technology, literature, art, sports or music — all the programs Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy offers to prepare young minds for college-prep high school. Share with me your dreams for your kids so I can build a school with your input.”
