Tuition Assistance

We believe that a Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy education should be financially accessible to any family in our parish communities who desires a Catholic education for their child[ren]. Therefore, substantial need-based tuition assistance is available for active parishioners of St. Rose, St. Anne, All Hallows, St. Peter and Immaculate Conception parishes.

Current tuition assistance received by Saint Patrick and John Paul II School families will be automatically extended to the 2010-2011 school year. Families whose circumstances have changed will need to reapply if additional assistance is needed.

Financial Aid packets will be available at Saint Patrick School and John Paul II School in March. The application process is completely confidential.

The deadline for application is April 15 for the following school year. Awards will be made based on need and the availability of funds. Families receiving financial aid must reapply yearly.

Applications are processed by the FACTS Grant & Aid Assessment Program. Applicants may submit a written application, or apply online at www.factstuitionaid.com.

FACTS helps Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy assess eligibility for financial aid, by standardizing the process and offering independent, objective financial analysis. Ultimately, award decisions are made by the governing board of Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy. Award notifications are made in May.

A $20 application processing fee payable to FACTS Grant & Aid needs to accompany the application. Families will be notified in May as to any tuition assistance that they may receive.

If you have any questions, please contact Laura Allen by phone at 916.421.4963.

To complete your application, you will need the following information:

  • Copies of your most recent IRS Federal Form 1040, or 1040A US Individual Income Tax Return, including all supporting tax schedules (C, E & F). FACTS does not accept State Tax returns.
  • Copies of your most recent W-2 Wage and Tax Statements for both you and your spouse.
  • Supporting documentation for household Social Security Income, Welfare, Child Support, Food Stamps, Worker’s Compensation, and TANF.
  • Payment of a nonrefundable $20 application fee.
