Tuition Assistance

We believe that a Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy education should be financially accessible to any family in our parish communities who desires a Catholic education for their child[ren]. Therefore, substantial need-based tuition assistance is available for active parishioners of St. Rose, St. Anne, All Hallows, St. Peter, Vietnamese Catholic Martyrs and Immaculate Conception parishes.

Financial Aid packets are available at Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy in the school office. The application process is completely confidential. An independent tuition assistance committee will determine the extent of tuition assistance that your family may receive.

The completed Financial Aid Application must be received in the school office by March 1 for the following school year. Awards will be made based on need and the availability of funds. Award notifications are made in mid - March. Families receiving financial aid must reapply yearly.

A $25 application processing fee payable to St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy needs to accompany the application. If you have any questions, please contact Lynn Lagomarsino by phone at 916.421.4963.

To complete your application, you will need the following information:

  • A completed Financial Aid Application.
  • Copies of your most recent IRS Federal Form 1040, or 1040A US Individual Income Tax Return, including all supporting tax schedules (C, E & F).
  • Copies of your most recent W-2 Wage and Tax Statements for both you and your spouse.
  • Supporting documentation for household Social Security Income, Welfare, Child Support, Food Stamps, Worker’s Compensation, and TANF.
  • Payment of a nonrefundable $25 application fee.
