Message From Leaders

The Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy begins a new era of providing a Pre-K-8th Catholic education to the St. Rose, St. Anne, All Hallows, St. Peter, and Immaculate Conception parish communities in South Sacramento. Centrally located to these communities, the mission of the SUCCEED Academy is to offer an excellent Catholic education focused on the academic and spiritual formation of young people from Catholic families living in South Sacramento. An innovative governance model, expert leadership, upgraded facilities, academic rigor, enhanced programs, and substantial tuition assistance are all hallmarks of the new Saint Patrick SUCCEED Academy. I encourage you to give strong consideration to enrolling your child for the coming school year. A Catholic school education is one of the most valuable gifts that you can give to your child in his or her lifetime.

+Jaime Soto
Bishop of Sacramento

A Word from the Principal

Dear Parents/Guardians.

The success of our students depends upon the teamwork among their teachers, their parents/guardians, and themselves. As with any team, the coach is essential to leading the team to success . . . God, our coach, is ever present with us . . .it takes the prayer of our teachers, our parents/guardians, and our students to assist each child to recognize their many gifts and talents and allow them to realize their strengths so that every effort can be made to insure their future success.

Sr. Mary will be starting an after school math club and will assist us with our Academic Decathlon team. We are grateful that Sr. Mary has agreed to become a member of our wonderful community.

Please continue to pray for all those people who are so involved with making St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy such a success!

We have so many people providing financial assistance to us each month to make the continuance of the wonderful programs we have a reality. We are blessed to have them in our lives!

Sincerely,
Laura Allen, Principal

Vision Statement

St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy will provide a gateway into 21st century learning by providing students with the tools to succeed as faith-filled, community-focused and self-motivated learners prepared to meet the needs of a diverse and changing world.

Mission Statement

St. Patrick SUCCEED Academy, a culturally diverse diocesan regional school, serves students in the South Sacramento area and the surrounding communities. The ministry of the Academy is threefold: to provide a positive, Christ-centered, 21st century learning experience through excellence in academics; to be attentive to all aspects of the individual child's development; and to provide students with the tools to be self-motivated critical thinkers. The modeling and practice of moral and ethical values, coupled with the teachings of our Catholic faith, further empower our students to meet all future challenges. Aiding parents in their responsibility as the primary educators of their children, the Academy strives to honor and celebrate our ethnic and spiritual diversity while preparing our students to be bearers of compassion, respect and knowledge to the larger world.
